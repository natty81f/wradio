About

W Radio was born out of a Los Angeles living room. Inspired by friends, music obsessions and conversations fueled by beer and Jack Daniel's, the idea of creating a platform for friends to share awesome tunes was a no-brainer. Hoping to inspire and be inspired, our mission is to connect and share with other musically minded individuals.
